Our Story

About Us

Mamori stands for PT. Manal Mamora Indonesia, was set out in 2017 to create and promote great-tasting and wide varieties of Indonesian coffee from upstream to downstream selection

Mamori is able to supply the coffee from many origins in Indonesia, and mostly selling the coffee sourced from Sumatera. We strive to grow our business with honesty, integrity and sustainability.

The Founder

M. NAUGAN SIMANUNGKALIT

N. Naugan Simanungkalit,has been involving in coffee business for more than 10 years, both in domestic and international market

She had served as USA Regional Manager of Coffindo Corporation, coffee exporter company based in Los Angeles, USA, and had participated in several Specialty Coffee Exhibition in US and Japan (SCAA & SCAJ), also in charge at the Foreign Affair Department of AEKI / AICE (The Association of Indonesian Coffee Exporters), and involved in Asia Coffee Symposium event. Recently she also served as Director of PT. Biji Kopi Internusa, the subsidiary company of Sinarmas. Prior to join Coffindo, she served as Public Relation Coordinator at JW Marriott Hotel for Food and Beverage Department, and the Representative from North Sumatera, Indonesia for SSEAYP (The Ship for South East Asian- Japanese Youth Program), which involve 11 countries.
