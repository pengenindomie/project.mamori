The Coffee Leaf / Teh Daun Kopi

Tea is easily accepted by wider segment as for women and children, compared to coffee.

Therefore Kahwa or Tea made of coffee leaf is also included to complete tbe philosophy behind ALFEE brand. At ALFEE we only carry Kahwa as our tea selection and serve it at ALFEE coffee shops, distribute it to the retail stores, as well as export it. Kahwa has tastier flavor than Cascara and has longer shelf life than regular tea.

Several studies have shown that coffee leaf tea to be beneficial in lowering hypertension, diabetes, and high antioxidant.

We work closely with farmer co-operation especially the farmer’s wife in purpose of empowering women, adding farmers’ additional income if there is no coffee cherries picking harvest.

Manfaat Utama:

  • Dikenal bermanfaat bagi metabolisme dan sistem kekebalan tubuh.
  • Mengandung kafein lebih rendah.
  • Membantu memberikan pendapatan tambahan bagi petani mitra kami.
